## ~에, ~에서, ~부터, ~까지, ~(으)로 

### N + 에 = at, in, on, to (for places)

### N + 에서 = at, in, from (for places)

#### at, in difference 
* 애서 on action verbs 
* 에   on none/action verbs

### N + 부터 from, since for time

### N + 까지 to, by, until

### N + (으)로 to by the means of / with, by, on
